2312 Essay

This essay was the final project for my high school AP English Literature and Composition class. The paper is about the book 2312 by Kim Stanley Robinson, a science fiction book detailing the state of the solar system leading up to that year. I analyzed the economies in the story and compared it to modern (late 2010s) economies, noting the author's beliefs and reasoning for what the future may hold and what the future should be.

Phyrnna Plaque

This 3D printed plaque was dedicated to Phyrnna, one of my favorite little-known music producers growing up. The flipped treble clef with wings is her logo and the blue play button represents YouTube, where she hosts her music. The wings in this design "flap" up and down together. The wings and treble clef can be printed in glow-in-the-dark filament for an awesome nighttime glow effect.
