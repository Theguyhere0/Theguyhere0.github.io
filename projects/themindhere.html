Themindhere Podcast

About the Podcast

Themindhere is a biweekly podcast hosted by yours truly. In past episodes, I've provided updates and insight into my YouTube channel content, followed by some various thoughts that I had throughout the weeks. Following a change in channel purpose and personal goals, this podcast has become a platform for me to voice my ideas about new knowledge I've learned, share new insights, and express my opinions about the current state of humanity.

This podcast started out as an exercise of my speaking abilities, but over time has grown to a platform for a genuine conversation with the outside world in a way that wasn't available before. For much of the podcast history, I've treated it as a hobby project, but I've began to see it as a serious medium of expression. To this day, I have yet to monetize the podcast. The goal of the podcast is to practice my communication skills as well as spark some interesting and mindful conversations about society. If my audience grows significantly, I might be open to sponsorship but I will never put conventional ads into any of my works. I aim not to extract value from those around me but to give and bring out value for others. This podcast is just one way I will try to do so.
